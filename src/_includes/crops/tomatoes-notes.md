<p>
  In cooler summers, focus on early varieties and consistent warmth. If nights stay cool, growth slows even if your calendar looks “on time”.
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Choose 55–70 day varieties for reliability.</li>
  <li>Transplant only after soil warms and nights are stable.</li>
  <li>Plan your fall cutoff using your first frost date.</li>
</ul>
